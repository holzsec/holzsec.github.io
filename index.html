About

Hi everyone üëã - I am Florian and I am a last year PhD student at the University of Vienna's Security & Privacy Research Group (SEC) and the Network & Critical Infrastructure Security Group (ERIS) at SBA-Research.

During my master studies I joined Team ERIS as a junior researcher. With my supervisor Johanna, we found an entry point for Internet-measurements in Austria (aim.sba-research.org). I am now pursuing a PhD in that field with the focus on measuring Internet standard adoption and compliance.

My research focuses on tracking protocol adoption and security, the transition to IPv6, and leveraging active measurements to detect Internet outages.

To make my measurements accessible to everyone, I try to build open measurement platforms. My first one, email-security-scans.org, is designed to rank email servers and providers for improved transparency and security.
